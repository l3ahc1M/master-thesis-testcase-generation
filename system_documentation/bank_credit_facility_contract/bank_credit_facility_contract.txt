A frame contract that defines an overall credit line for a hierarchy of subordinated bank contracts.The subordinated bank contracts can be bank account contracts (drawings) of different kinds of products (for example, current account and loan) having multiple contract currencies. It is also possible to subordinate other facilities, thus building a complex contractual hierarchy.
You can use this API to query and read bank credit facility contracts.Each Bank Credit Facility Contract contains additional information like the main business partner (borrower) or participation conditions which serve as a restriction towards the allowed subordinated bank contracts. A bank master contract is a frame contract between a bank and its customer that is based on a hierarchy of participating bank contracts. Bank master contracts and the associated hierarchies are set up for features such as combined settlement, combined statement and so on.Bank Credit Facility Contract is a type of a bank master contract.
