An authorization given by a debtor to a creditor to initiate instructions to debit funds owed to the creditor from a specified account, and the authorization to the debtor bank to comply with the instructions issued by the creditor.
You can use this API to create new direct debit mandates or to query existing direct debit mandates.Remark:
The service Direct Debit Mandate is assigned to service group Transactional Banking.General:
The properties of the EDM that are based on a code GDT do not contain supplementary components (e.g. listID) that are known from the GDTs of the enterprise services.
