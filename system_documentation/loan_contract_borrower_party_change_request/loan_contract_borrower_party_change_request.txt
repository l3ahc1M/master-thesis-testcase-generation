A request from the borrower to a lender (financial institution) to change the borrower of a loan contract immediately. A loan contract borrower party change request also supports the transfer of due items from the previous borrower to the new borrower.Note:
The entity data model for loan contract borrower party change request contains a subset of the attributes of the business object of loan contract borrower party change request.Remark:
The service Loan Contract Borrower Party Change Request is assigned to service group Transactional Banking.Use:
A Loan Contract Borrower Party Change Request is used to propose, create and display borrower party change requests. The propose loan contract borrower party change request is used to query default values for reason code, correspondence required indicators for new and former borrower party, charge waiver requested indicator and additional data like activity codes and checklist item codes for creation of a loan contract borrower party change request.
